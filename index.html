About me

Code, design,
& a bit of music.

"18 years old, in my final year of secondary school — already shipping software people use."

I'm Cedric Mupenzi — aka Cooper — a Rwandan developer and graphic designer based in Kigali. I got into software through pure curiosity, built skills through relentless hands-on building, and never stopped.

My edge is the combination: I can write clean backend logic, ship a polished React frontend, and make it look intentionally designed — not just assembled. That's rare. Most developers can't design; most designers can't code.

Outside the editor I'm into music and finance. That's not incidental — it's why one of my projects is a family money tracker. When something bothers me, I build a fix. That's the mindset I bring to every project.
